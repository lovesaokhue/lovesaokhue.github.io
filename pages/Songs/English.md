## Grades 1–3: The Foundation

*(Focus: Phonemic awareness, basic vocabulary, clear vowels, and physical rhythm)*

### Grade 1

1. **"The ABC Song"** – Target: Letter identification and core phonics.
2. **"Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star"** – Target: Simple rhyming pairs (*star/are*, *high/sky*).
3. **"Head, Shoulders, Knees, and Toes"** – Target: Anatomy nouns linked directly to physical actions.
4. **"Old MacDonald Had a Farm"** – Target: Animal vocabulary, sentence repetition, and basic onomatopoeia.
5. **"If You're Happy and You Know It"** – Target: Primary emotional adjectives and immediate action imperatives.
6. **"The Wheels on the Bus"** – Target: Everyday transport nouns and rhythmic pacing.
7. **"The Rainbow Song"** – Target: Smooth color identification and simple sequence recall.
8. **"Ten in the Bed"** – Target: Counting backwards and the foundational phrasal verb *roll over*.
9. **"Rain, Rain, Go Away"** – Target: Simple request frameworks and weather terms.
10. **"Baby Shark"** – Target: Immediate family nouns (*mommy, daddy, grandma*) with intense phonetic repetition.

### Grade 2

1. **"Days of the Week Song"** – Target: Sequential calendar vocabulary and capitalization concepts.
2. **"Months of the Year Chant"** – Target: Clear multi-syllable pronunciation of the twelve months.
3. **"The Hokey Pokey"** – Target: Directional prepositions and left vs. right body coordination.
4. **"Five Little Monkeys"** – Target: Basic numerical subtraction and introductory past-tense verbs (*bumped, said*).
5. **"Row, Row, Row Your Boat"** – Target: Blending liquid consonants (*r, l*) and the continuous adverb *merrily*.
6. **"Do You Like Broccoli Ice Cream?"** – Target: Constructing basic questions, likes, and dislikes.
7. **"This Is the Way We Wash Our Face"** – Target: Present simple tense used to describe daily morning hygiene routines.
8. **"Walking in the Jungle"** – Target: Action verbs, active counting, and wild animal classifications.
9. **"The Clean Up Song"** – Target: Shared classroom directives and household vocabulary.
10. **"Skidamarink"** – Target: Affection idioms and times of day (*morning, afternoon, evening*).

### Grade 3

1. **"You Are My Sunshine"** – Target: Expressive emotional language and soft metaphorical imagery (*skies are gray*).
2. **"Edelweiss"** (*The Sound of Music*) – Target: Sustained, gentle vowel production and simple nature adjectives.
3. **"Do-Re-Mi"** (*The Sound of Music*) – Target: Phonetic building blocks and connecting abstract musical notes to concrete nouns.
4. **"What a Wonderful World"** (Louis Armstrong) – Target: Visual descriptors, natural world imagery, and stable present simple frameworks.
5. **"Bingo"** – Target: Auditory working memory, spelling coordination, and steady, punctuated pacing.
6. **"Puff, the Magic Dragon"** – Target: Whimsical past-tense storytelling and imaginative narrative comprehension.
7. **"This Land Is Your Land"** – Target: Possessive pronouns (*your, my*) and foundational geographical vocabulary.
8. **"Over the Rainbow"** – Target: Expressions of hope and introducing the concept of abstract adverbs (*somewhere*).
9. **"My Favorite Things"** (*The Sound of Music*) – Target: Rapid listing of compound nouns and crisp, descriptive imagery.
10. **"Polly Wolly Doodle"** – Target: Fast-paced rhyming strings, rhythm mastery, and playful nonsense syllables.
