## The Primary School Block (Grades 1–5)

*Focus: Phonetics, basic tones, concrete nouns, everyday actions, and simple sentence structures.*

### Grade 1: Foundational Sounds & Simple Nouns

*Linguistic focus: High repetition, clear tone distinctions, basic pronouns (bà, mẹ, con), and common animal/object names.*

* **Một Con Vịt** – Excellent for practicing basic numbers and foundational verbs (*xòe, kêu, gặp, vẫy*).
* **Cả Nhà Thương Nhau** – Teaches the core vocabulary of family relationships and basic emotional verbs (*thương, ghét, nhớ*).
* **Rửa Mặt Như Mèo** – Great for playful onomatopoeia and learning basic body parts and hygiene actions.
* **Tập Đếm** – A direct tool for mastering numbers 1 to 10 with a steady, rhythmic cadence.
* **Hai Con Thằn Lằn Con** – Simple narrative structure that introduces cause-and-effect clauses.
* **Cháu Yêu Bà** – Focuses on adjectives of appearance (*trắng, tóc bạc*) and simple expressions of affection.
* **Bóng Tròn To** – Perfect for learning basic shapes, sizes, and physical descriptions (*to, nhỏ, tròn*).
* **Kìa Con Bướm Vàng** – Set to the familiar tune of "Frère Jacques," making it easy to focus purely on Vietnamese pronunciation.
* **Đi Học Về** – Teaches standard cultural greetings and politeness markers used upon entering or leaving a home.
* **Đếm Sao** – Introduces natural elements (*sao, trời*) alongside rhythmic counting patterns.

### Grade 2: Expanding Actions & Visual Descriptions

*Linguistic focus: Expanded verb phrases, simple adjectives, and introduction to conditional phrases.*

* **Chú Ếch Con** – Explores rich descriptive adjectives (*ngoan, xinh, tròn*) and early nature vocabulary.
* **Con Heo Đất** – Teaches vocabulary related to personal habits, saving money, and affectionate verbs.
* **Chị Ong Nâu Và Em Bé** – Introduces compound words (*ong nâu, mặt trời, máy bay*) and polite modes of address.
* **Chú Voi Con Ở Bản Đôn** – Great for regional vocabulary (*Bản Đôn, Tây Nguyên*) and physical traits (*vòi, đuôi, tai*).
* **Ba Ngọn Nến Lung Linh** – Introduces structural metaphors (*thắp sáng, một gia đình*) using daily objects.
* **Mẹ Ơi Có Biết** – Focuses on simple question formats and expressing personal inner thoughts.
* **Đội Kèn Tí Hon** – Excellent for learning musical vocabulary and action verbs related to movement.
* **Tập Thể Dục Buổi Sáng** – Connects simple verbs directly with physical movements (*hít thở, giơ tay*).
* **Cho Con** – Introduces gentle, poetic sentence structures regarding parental love and long-term wishes.
* **Chị Hằng** – Introduces cultural folklore vocabulary relating to the Moon and the Mid-Autumn Festival.

### Grade 3: Daily Routines & Social Connections

*Linguistic focus: Introduction to compound verbs, school environments, and seasonal changes.*

* **Đi Học (Bùi Đình Thảo)** – Masterful integration of nature vocabulary (*hương cốm, rừng vắng, suối*) with daily routines.
* **Ngày Đầu Tiên Đi Học** – Explores past-tense recollection and words associated with complex emotional states (*bỡ ngỡ, khóc, vỗ về*).
* **Em Yêu Trường Em** – Packed with school-related concrete nouns (*bàn, ghế, sách, vở, thầy cô, bạn bè*).
* **Bắc Kim Thang** – A traditional folk rhyme great for practicing faster phonetic delivery and vernacular naming conventions.
* **Trống Cơm** – Introduces traditional instruments (*trống cơm, đàn bầu*) and distinct rhythmic phrasing.
* **Bàn Tay Mẹ** – Focuses on self-sacrificing action verbs (*chăm, nấu, ủ, ấm*) and close-knit family bonds.
* **Chiếc Đèn Ông Sao** – Traditional Mid-Autumn vocabulary featuring complex spatial and celestial descriptors.
* **Thằng Cuội** – Introduces early metaphorical storytelling surrounding Vietnamese lunar myths.
* **Quả Gì** – Structured entirely as a riddle game, perfect for practicing conversational question-and-answer patterns.
* **Trái Đất Này Là Của Chúng Mình** – Expores global concepts, colors, and basic inclusive pronouns (*chúng mình, chúng ta*).

### Grade 4: Descriptive Elements & Abstract Emotions

*Linguistic focus: Time markers, comparative language, and basic abstract emotional adjectives.*

* **Bụi Phấn** – Introduces deep cultural values surrounding mentorship alongside delicate imagery (*bạc tóc, rơi rơi*).
* **Mái Trường Mến Yêu** – Focuses on longer compound sentences and peaceful spatial transitions (*nhẹ nhàng, xôn xao*).
* **Hạt Gạo Làng Ta** – Rich in agricultural vocabulary (*bão tháng bảy, mưa tháng ba, giọt mồ hôi*) and hard-work metaphors.
* **Hổng Dám Đâu** – A highly conversational pop song filled with colloquial modern expressions and teenage dilemmas.
* **Em Là Hoa Hồng Nhỏ** – A classic Trịnh Công Sơn piece for children, utilizing simple but beautifully poetic compound nouns.
* **Con Cào Cào** – Focuses on health, fitness, and energetic lifestyle verbs (*thích thể thao, bay cao*).
* **Tia Nắng Hạt Mưa** – Uses creative personification to contrast elements of nature against human personality traits.
* **Reo Vang Bình Minh** – Exceptional for practicing continuous, lyrical descriptions of dawn and nature sounds.
* **Chiều Nay Em Đi Câu Cá** – Packed with humorous conversational structures, familial teasing, and rural hobbies.
* **Cánh Én Tuổi Thơ** – Explores simple metaphors connecting changing seasons with hope and collective friendship.

### Grade 5: Complex Sentences & Cultural Foundations

*Linguistic focus: Cohesive conjunctions (vì...nên, tuy...nhưng), foundational social values, and narrative depth.*

* **Màu Áo Chú Bộ Đội** – Introduces themes of national defense, respect, and compound color/attire descriptors.
* **Bông Hồng Cài Áo** – A deeply touching piece that explores complex emotional debt, gratitude, and abstract love.
* **Khăn Quàng Thắp Sáng Bình Minh** – Filled with energetic vocabulary of forward movement, collective action, and youth organization.
* **Trưa Hè Ngắt Cánh Bằng Lăng** – Evocative summer-themed vocabulary highlighting school nostalgia and seasonal shifts.
* **Hành Khúc Tới Trường** – Introduces march-style cadence, requiring precise, energetic pronunciation of sharp consonants.
* **Tiếng Ve Gọi Hè** – Focuses on nature sounds, onomatopoeia, and transition states of time.
* **Em Đi Trong Tươi Xanh** – Rich in idealistic imagery of the homeland, peace, and hopeful futuristic thinking.
* **Tuổi Thơ Hạ Long** – Explores maritime, coastal, and geographical vocabulary unique to northern Vietnamese landscapes.
* **Trống Ca Lên Đường** – Introduces motivational vocabulary, legacy themes, and high-energy call-and-response patterns.
* **Ước Mơ Xanh** – Focuses on environmental concepts, future ambitions, and abstract internal desires.

## The Middle School Block (Grades 6–9)

*Focus: Folk-inspired structures, advanced compound adjectives, historical themes, and early metaphorical interpretations.*

### Grade 6: Poetic Expressions & Human Nature

*Linguistic focus: Transitioning to adult prose, poetic syntax inversion, and emotional metaphors.*

* **Mặt Trời Bé Con** – Gentle storytelling using simple everyday objects as vast symbols of imagination and hope.
* **Tuổi Đời Mênh Mông** – Introduces expansive nature imagery (*mây qua đầu, cây thắp đèn*) applied to human growth phases.
* **Bức Họa Đồng Quê** – A joyful masterclass in pastoral vocabulary, rural industries, and vivid soundscapes.
* **Ca Ngợi Tổ Quốc** – Grand, formal vocabulary focusing on geography, mountains, rivers, and national pride.
* **Tiếng Chuông Và Ngọn Cờ** – Explores advanced themes of peace, global harmony, and anti-war terminology.
* **Cho Bạn Cho Tôi** – Focuses on friendship transitions, supportive emotional language, and teenage milestones.
* **Tiếng Chim Trong Vườn Bác** – Explores reverence, respectful terms of endearment, and specific bird and plant names.
* **Lí Cây Đa** – Introduces classic northern folk metrics, rhythmic vocal fillers (*bên đình, tình rằng*), and traditional courtship phrases.
* **Qua Cầu Gió Bay** – Focuses on wordplay, clothing vocabulary from historical eras, and classic southern/central colloquialisms.
* **Trở Về Dòng Sông Tuổi Thơ** – Rich, deeply nostalgic prose detailing childhood connections to landscape, water, and passage of time.

### Grade 7: Folk Heritage & Regional Dialects

*Linguistic focus: Regional idioms, poetic meters (Lục Bát), traditional life philosophy, and passive structures.*

* **Bèo Dạt Mây Trôi** – The pinnacle of Quan Họ folk phrasing; exceptional for understanding longing, separation, and nature symbols.
* **Inh Lả Ơ** – A short Northwest folk melody introducing ethnic minority styles of greeting and natural beauty.
* **Đi Cấy** – Traditional rhythmic counting song focused on agricultural solidarity and historical communal work structures.
* **Lí Đĩa Bánh Bò** – A vibrant Southern folk song perfect for practicing Southern pronunciation shifts and regional culinary names.
* **Cây Trúc Xinh** – Teaches the subtle art of indirect comparison and traditional values of grace and inner beauty.
* **Quê Hương** – Set to a famous poem, this song is ideal for learning lyrical similes (*quê hương là chùm khế ngọt*).
* **Đất Nước Lời Ru** – Explores ancient historical contexts, early mythology, and the metaphorical weight of a mother’s lullaby.
* **Tình Ca Tây Bắc** – Rich in mountain geography, specialized regional metaphors (*rừng ban, nương lúa*), and deep romantic dialogue.
* **Trường Sơn Đông Trường Sơn Tây** – Masterpiece of wartime poetry contrast, showing advanced usage of parallel structures (*bên nắng đốt, bên mưa quây*).
* **Nhạc Rừng** – Uses high-level onomatopoeia to recreate jungle soundscapes, turning nature sounds into active verbs.

### Grade 8: Contemporary Narratives & Deep Nuance

*Linguistic focus: Modern narrative structures, reflective essays in musical form, and complex emotional states.*

* **Nhớ Về Hà Nội** – Beautiful urban storytelling filled with distinct historical landmarks, street scenes, and sensory memory words.
* **Mong Ước Kỷ Niệm Xưa** – The definitive graduation anthem; ideal for mastering vocabulary related to memories, time, and regret.
* **Vào Hạ** – Optimistic modern pop prose that contrasts personal freedom against the heavy, routine burdens of daily life.
* **Xe Đạp** – A contemporary ballad filled with subtle conversational nuance, unspoken young romance, and soft pop phonetics.
* **Sống Như Những Đóa Hoa** – Highly motivational vocabulary focusing on individual purpose, social empathy, and resilience.
* **Em Trong Mắt Tôi** – Rich in descriptive words celebrating modern Vietnamese women, the traditional *Áo Dài*, and natural grace.
* **Tôi Thấy Hoa Vàng Trên Cỏ Xanh** – Mystical, poetic imagery capturing innocent perspective, memory lapses, and serene landscapes.
* **Cha Và Con Gái** – Heartfelt dialogue-style lyrics focusing on familial protection, advice, and unconditional guidance.
* **Nhật Ký Của Mẹ** – An epic timeline song tracking a child's life, perfect for learning vocabulary of physical and emotional development.
* **Cho Tôi Xin Một Vé Đi Tuổi Thơ** – Captures colloquial nostalgia, adult regrets, and playful childhood slang.

### Grade 9: Intellectual Themes & Social Ideals

*Linguistic focus: Sino-Vietnamese abstract words (tự nguyện, khát vọng), socio-political concepts, and formal philosophical thought.*

* **Khát Vọng** – Explores advanced existential verbs (*sống, hiến dâng*) through heavy parallel natural symbols (*nền đá, phù sa, cánh chim*).
* **Tự Nguyện** – A poignant look at selflessness and civic duty using simple but incredibly profound conditional grammar.
* **Biển Hát Chiều Nay** – Advanced maritime prose that beautifully links sea geography with internal emotional landscapes.
* **Bài Ca Không Quên** – Deeply emotional wartime reflection, rich with vocabulary of historical struggle, memory, and loyalty.
* **Huyền Thoại Mẹ** – Trịnh Công Sơn’s historical masterpiece honoring mothers using highly advanced, evocative war-era metaphors.
* **Giấc Mơ Trưa** – Ethereal, abstract sensory vocabulary focusing on silence, space, light, and dreamy subconscious states.
* **Nồng Nàn Hà Nội** – High-energy modern urban descriptions focusing on the blending of ancient heritage with fast modern lifestyles.
* **Giọt Sương Trên Mí Mắt** – Explores poetic expressions of vulnerability, emotional longing, and hidden sadness.
* **Hoa Cỏ Mùa Xuân** – Celebrates renewal, vibrant visual adjectives, and the abstract internal shifts brought by spring.
* **Nối Vòng Tay Lớn** – The ultimate song of unity; invaluable for learning high-level community, geographical, and political bonding terms.

## The High School Block (Grades 10–12)

*Focus: Classical literature connections, existential philosophy, complex metaphors, idiom integration, and deep Sino-Vietnamese conceptual roots.*

### Grade 10: Literary Allusions & Modern Prose

*Linguistic focus: Romantic literary realism, extended poetic metaphors, and contemporary everyday idiomatic structures.*

* **Hương Thầm** – Based on a famous poem; explores unspoken love during wartime through subtle olfactory and natural metaphors.
* **Chân Quê** – Adapted from Nguyễn Bính’s famous poem; essential for exploring historical rural values versus urban changes.
* **Phượng Hồng** – Filled with high-level high school romance terminology, specific literary symbolism, and nostalgic prose.
* **Tình Thơ** – Excellent for understanding the vocabulary of structural transitions, parting gifts, and lifelong friendships.
* **Để Gió Cuốn Đi** – Trịnh Công Sơn’s philosophical advice on altruism, focusing on zen-like vocabulary and acceptance.
* **Mỗi Ngày Tôi Chọn Một Niềm Vui** – Excellent daily routine language shifted toward mindful, intentional living and emotional choice.
* **Tuổi Hồng Thơ Ngây** – A classic folk-pop tragic ballad that highlights conditional past tenses and dramatic emotional declarations.
* **Hồn Quê** – Rich in traditional southern sensory words, rural culinary arts, and descriptions of ancestral roots.
* **Ông Bà Anh** – Contrasts historical courting language and simple romance with modern technological/social media slang.
* **Đi Về Nhà** – A brilliant masterclass in modern rhythmic delivery, home nostalgia, and highly authentic everyday Vietnamese idioms.

### Grade 11: Advanced Aesthetics & Socio-Cultural Ideals

*Linguistic focus: Artistic prose, abstract cultural landscapes, complex adjectival pairings, and historical philosophy.*

* **Đất Nước (Phạm Minh Tuấn)** – Deeply patriotic and literary; incorporates historical myths, agricultural struggles, and sacrifices.
* **Giai Điệu Tổ Quốc** – High-level artistic vocabulary that translates abstract sounds of history and nature into musical phrases.
* **Thành Phố Trẻ** – Focuses on post-war reconstruction terminology, dynamic urban energy, and youthful forward progress.
* **Ngẫu Hứng Lý Qua Cầu** – A clever deconstruction of folk motifs, showcasing ironic wordplay and deep Central/Southern cultural quirks.
* **Chị Tôi (Trần Tiến)** – Tragic narrative prose utilizing high-level cultural subtexts regarding traditional duty and family roles.
* **Hà Nội Mùa Vắng Những Cơn Mưa** – Explores mood-setting adjectives, atmospheric descriptions, and romantic urban isolation.
* **Thu Vàng (Cung Tiến)** – Masterpiece of pre-war neo-classical composition; introduces incredibly rare, advanced literary syntax and imagery.
* **Giọt Mưa Thu** – A deeply melancholy classic; exceptional for studying historic grief metaphors, rain imagery, and poetic desolation.
* **Ôi Quê Tôi** – Strikingly raw, avant-garde folk-pop lyrics detailing mystical rural rituals, ancestral lands, and spiritual connections.
* **Về Quê (Phó Đức Phương)** – Packed with rich Northern cultural references, folk beliefs, and deep yearnings for heritage landscapes.

### Grade 12: Philosophy, Existentialism & Linguistic Mastery

*Linguistic focus: Existential vocabulary (cát bụi, hư vô, cội nguồn), profound double-entendres, and the peak of poetic/literary Vietnamese expression.*

* **Diễm Xưa** – Masterpiece of atmospheric longing; exceptional for studying abstract spatial relationships and poetic melancholy.
* **Hạ Trắng** – Deeply existential Trịnh Công Sơn piece exploring surrealism, life-and-death metaphors, and eternal devotion.
* **Cát Bụi** – Focuses heavily on Buddhist philosophy, impermanence, and advanced vocabulary relating to life cycles and the cosmos.
* **Chiếc Lá Thu Phai** – High-level introspective prose focusing on aging, acceptance, the passing of seasons, and self-reflection.
* **Tình Ca (Hoàng Việt)** – Peak wartime romance intertwined with geopolitical love; features high-level vocabulary of resilience and loyalty.
* **Tình Ca (Phạm Duy)** – An sweeping linguistic journey across the entire identity of the Vietnamese language, geography, and historical soul.
* **Áo Anh Sứt Chỉ Đường Tà** – Based on Hữu Loan's famous wartime poem; utilizes rare, visceral vocabulary of grief, battlefields, and marital tragedy.
* **Hòn Vọng Phu (Lê Thương)** – An epic three-part musical suite that is the ultimate test of historical vocabulary, legends, and classical prose mastery.
* **Dạ Cổ Hoài Lang** – The classic Southern *Đờn ca tài tử* foundational piece; perfect for mastering archaic literary terms of devotion and late-night longing.
* **Khúc Thụy Du** – A profound, raw look at love amidst existential chaos, utilizing dense, complex modern poetic syntax.
